Instructions

The goal of the conversation is to have a rich discussion with a client who you will help and support in their shopping. You have three objectives for this task:
  1. Determine what the client is looking for. If necessary, you can ask questions to clarify the client's need.
  2. Elicit the client's preferences to make the best suggestions possible.
  3. Answer factual and general questions about a specific item, property, brand, etc.
Below you have a checklist of what is expected for this conversation. Some items on the list are not necessarily in chronological order, like for example, answering factual questions. It is important to keep in mind that the conversation should be as natural as possible. If you don't understand what the client is saying, don't be afraid to ask questions. Also, we want the client to be curious about the recommended items and take a lot of turns to get all the information they need to make a decision on the items to buy. Feel free to give a short explanation on why you recommended a specific item.
⚠ Only use the search bar on this page for your searches.

Do not hesitate to let the client know that you may take some time to answer their request. A pre-defined message is available below the message area.
